ludum dare projects

Ludum Dare is a huge "game jam" community which happens every few months. Thousands of indie developers come together and pledge a weekend to creating a game from scratch, either individually or as a group. This is a collection of all the games I've made during this contest, and you can find more information about it yourself by visiting the website: http://ludumdare.com/compo/

Moon Harvest
Spring 2014
My first ludum dare game. I created a custom engine entirely from javascript+HTML5. It was an awesome time and really the foundation of most future ludum dare games that I made. The theme, as I recall, as "under the surface" or something like that. So in this game, you shoot off the surface of a planet in order to "mine" the resources.

Space Vikings
Fall 2014
I was feeling wholly uninspired during this ludum dare. After the first night I had decided to give up entirely and went to bed angry. The next morning I realized that was foolish and so I just threw ideas on a page and rolled with it. Apparently those ideas were "space" and "vikings". I'm not sure what the theme was, but all in all it was a fun time. My first attempt at a sort of resource management game.

SkyBlade
Spring 2015
My foray into using physics engines and bullet time. An arena-style survival game where you destroy the attacking zombies until you can survive no longer. I had always wanted to play with a physics engine though sadly, this game did not turn out how I wanted. I almost think it would have been better to write my own physics engine for all the time I wasted trying to just get the engine bootstrapped! But in the end, I did finish about as much as I wanted in the short time I had.
